# CONTAMINATION POLICY

Adopted 2026-07-18 (AMENDMENTS A & B, governance session). Referenced by
`research_register_v2.csv` conventions (row CONVENTION-0001). Binding on every
analysis in this workspace.

## Outcome-blind trial rule (AMENDMENT A — verbatim)

"Any analysis that observes strategy or signal outcomes conditional on the
signal — including event-study CARs, conditional return plots, and 'sanity
check' performance summaries — is a TRIAL and must be registered, regardless
of formality. Only outcome-blind diagnostics are free: rank-autocorrelation,
turnover/churn simulation, correlation and cointegration structure, capacity
math, timestamp/PIT audits, cost tables. If in doubt, it is a trial."

## Pool eligibility (AMENDMENT B — verbatim)

"The pre-registered pooled program NAV test may include only sleeves that
individually survived their shadow-book stage. Pooling aggregates survivors;
it never resurrects a killed or unfinished sleeve."
